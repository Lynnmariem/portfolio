About Me

During my studies, I developed an interest in exploring cognitive psychology in the context of human-computer interaction. This gave me a solid grounding in the principles of user experience. In many group projects, we researched how people interact with digital agents, social media, algorithms, and robots. I am passionate about creating. The Detta GameJams at the University of Duisburg-Essen provided me with the opportunity to brainstorm and design alongside others, producing small games in just 48 hours. Back in 2024, I took part in the GameJam by running a UI workshop and sharing my insights on UX.

Since 2023, I have worked as a student assistant in the central UX Product Team, specialising in UX design. During this time, I have gained practical UX experience through feedback sessions, customer data evaluation and research interviews, as well as creating an online training course on product discovery.
